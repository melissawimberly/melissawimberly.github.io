Today, I am going to be (very) briefly discussing how it felt to create my first website.

Creating my first website was quite an experience. To say the least, t was some kind of a roller coaster!

At times it was instantly gratifying, while at other times it was instantly frustrating! There were times when I wanted to share my webpage with everyone I knew, and there were times I wanted to throw my computer out the window!

Ultimately I learned more from jumping in and creating it, than reading about how to use HTML and CSS. Learning by doing seems to be the key with programming. However, I need to keep in mind that another huge part of program learning is practice. So I will need to make sure to keep practicing!
